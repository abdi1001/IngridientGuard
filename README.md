IngredientGuard helps you make faster, more informed food choices by turning a simple barcode scan into clear ingredient and nutrition insight.

Scan packaged food products with your iPhone camera and instantly review ingredients, declared allergens, and nutrition details in one place. IngredientGuard is built for people who want a quicker way to check products against their personal food preferences, sensitivities, and ingredient watchlists.

With IngredientGuard, you can:

Scan food barcodes in seconds
Review ingredient lists in a cleaner, easier-to-read format
See declared allergens and traces when product data is available
Check nutrition highlights before you buy or eat
Keep a personal watchlist of ingredients you want to avoid
Flag possible ingredient matches based on your saved profile
Revisit recent scans for faster repeat lookups
IngredientGuard is designed to help you shop with more confidence, compare products more easily, and stay aware of what is in the foods you bring home.

Important: IngredientGuard provides helpful product data and ingredient screening tools, but it is not a medical app and should not replace professional medical advice or label verification. Always review product packaging and consult a qualified professional for allergy- or health-related decisions.
