Privacy Policy

IngredientGuard Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 2, 2026

IngredientGuard helps users scan food barcodes and review product information such as ingredients, allergens, and nutrition details. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app may collect, how it is used, and the choices available to users.

Information We Collect

  • Barcode searches you enter or scan in the app.
  • Ingredient watchlists, allergy preferences, and related settings you save in the app.
  • Recent scan history stored to improve repeat lookups and app usability.
  • Basic technical data required to operate the service, such as app requests to the backend.

How We Use Information

  • To return product results for scanned or entered barcodes.
  • To compare product ingredients against the preferences or watchlists you save.
  • To maintain app functionality, troubleshoot issues, and improve performance.

Product Data Sources

IngredientGuard may display product information obtained from third-party food and product databases. That information is provided for convenience and should always be verified against the product packaging.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. Information may be processed by service providers only as needed to operate the app, host backend services, or support subscriptions and app distribution.

Data Retention

Saved preferences and scan history may remain available until you delete the app, clear app data, or otherwise request deletion where applicable.

Children's Privacy

IngredientGuard is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Your Choices

  • You can choose whether to scan products or enter barcodes manually.
  • You can edit or remove your saved ingredient preferences inside the app.
  • You can stop using the app at any time and remove the app from your device.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact support@abdifatahahmed.com.
